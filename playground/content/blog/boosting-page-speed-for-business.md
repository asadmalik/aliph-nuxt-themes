Website performance is one of the most critical elements of digital success. Speed affects everything from user behavior to search ranking to conversion rates. Studies show that even a one-second delay can dramatically reduce engagement. For businesses, this means lost revenue and diminished trust. Tools such as [PageSpeed Insights](https://developers.google.com/speed/pagespeed/insights/) and [GTMetrix](https://gtmetrix.com) make it easier to monitor and improve performance across various devices and network conditions.

## Why Page Speed Matters  
In a competitive digital landscape, users expect websites to load instantly. A fast-loading site not only improves user satisfaction but also signals professionalism and technical competence. Browsers reward well-optimized websites by loading them more efficiently, and search engines prioritize them in rankings.

Several technical improvements can significantly reduce load time and increase performance scores.

### Optimize Media Assets

#### Lazy Loading
